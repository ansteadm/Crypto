How to Buy Any CryptoCurrency:

Step 1: Pick One of the Three Methods Below to Buy BTC, ETH, or LTC:

Purchase with Coinbase: Go to Coinbase.com create an account, add a payment method then buy either BTC, ETH or LTC. FYI, if you spend over $100 dollars while using my link you will get a $10 bonus! Also, for planning ahead to make it easier to get Alt Coins such as Ripple, IoTa, Verge, and ADA, I would highly recommend purchasing with ETH because transferring the money is faster and cheaper than with BTC, and less transactions than LTC.

Step 2: Pick a Crypto Currency Trading Platform:

Crypto Exchange “Binance”: Once you have your BTC, ETH or LTC (preferably ETH), go to Binance.com and create your account “No Verification Needed” -meaning just input your name, and an email and you'll be set. By using this link you will get a 50% discount on all your trading fees :). After creating your account you will need to transfer your Coins that you just purchased to your Binance wallet. First you need to download the Authy app on your smartphone for certifying that it is your account. After this go to binance and hover over the tab "Funds" at the top, and then select "Deposit Withdrawals". From here, select your corresponding cryptocurrency that you purchased, or already have on coinbase. If you don't select the same one your money can disappear! Click on "Deposit" on the right side of your screen, copy the address and paste it on the send address in your Coinbase account. Now, just press send! After your Coins have reached your Binance wallet (which can take some time, so please be patient!) go to the exchange and look for the Crypto Currency you would like to purchase, then buy however many you want. Ideally when purchasing cryptos you want to buy during a dip in price, they can be extremely volatile!
